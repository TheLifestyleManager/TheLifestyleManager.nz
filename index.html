FAQs

Frequently asked questions

A few common questions about how I work. If you don't see your question here, you're always welcome to ask.

Who do I work best with?

I work best with people who have plenty of responsibility and limited bandwidth: high performers, business owners, elders who feel out of their depth with technology, busy parents, and households with a lot of moving parts.

If you've reached the point where doing everything yourself is costing you time, energy or peace of mind, this service is likely a good fit.

Do you work in-person, online, or both?

Both. I'm based in Ōtautahi / Christchurch and can work in-person locally, as well as online with clients across Aotearoa and beyond.

Many things can be handled remotely (research, tech support, planning, admin), and for certain clients or projects we can arrange in-person visits or travel as needed.

What are your hours and availability?

My availability is flexible and depends on the client and the arrangement we create. For ongoing support, we'll agree on clear windows where you can expect fast responses, and when I'm offline.

I put my phone on airplane mode at night, but outside of that, I'm generally responsive and present. We'll set expectations together so you know exactly what to count on.

What won't you do?

I don't do cleaning or heavy physical labour.

My focus is on problem solving, logistics, tech, planning, research, coordination, and being a calm, capable extra mind and pair of hands in your life. If something sits outside my scope, I'll help you find the right person for it.

How does pricing work?

For one-off jobs, I usually quote a simple hourly or fixed fee once I understand the scope. For ongoing support, we can choose a monthly package that matches the level of involvement you need.

You can see example packages and pricing on the Services & packages page. We can always adjust the details after an initial conversation.
