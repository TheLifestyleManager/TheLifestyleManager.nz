For a short time, I’m offering half-price support for new clients at $60/hr — with no minimum hours. It’s a simple way to see how it feels to have someone take tasks off your plate.

Who this is for

This intro offer is ideal if you:

  • Have a growing list of jobs you never quite get to

  • Are curious what it’s like to have a lifestyle manager, without a big commitment

What you can use it for

During your intro hours, we can work on things like:

1) Book a free 20-minute call so I can understand what you need and whether I’m the right fit.

3) We decide together what to prioritise first, so you see progress quickly.
