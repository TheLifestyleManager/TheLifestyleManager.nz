Services & packages

Every client is different. Some people need a short burst of support to untangle a mess. Others want an ongoing extra mind and pair of hands in their life. This page gives you a clear picture of what we can do together.

Services

How I can support you

Clear, calming delivery inspired by luxury hospitality and discreet concierge teams.

Life logistics & everyday admin

For when the “small stuff” is no longer small. I take on the tasks that drain your energy so you can focus on what matters.

  • Inbox triage, bills, renewals and subscriptions
  • Researching and comparing products, services or providers
  • Coordinating tradespeople, deliveries and appointments
  • Helping untangle long to-do lists into clear priorities

Tech & digital support

For people who don't have the patience or desire to keep up with tech – or who want someone they trust to make smart decisions for them.

  • Choosing and setting up phones, laptops and home technology
  • Sorting internet plans, providers and upgrades
  • Setting up and organising accounts, backups and passwords
  • Guided support for AI tools and apps so they work for you, not against you

Travel, projects & experiences

For when you want things done properly, without having to manage every detail yourself.

  • Planning and booking trips, retreats and time away
  • Creating full itineraries with transport, accommodation and activities
  • Project management for personal or small business projects
  • Event and experience coordination in collaboration with your team

Elder support & advocacy

Patient, practical support for elders and tech-averse clients who want to feel safer and more capable in a changing world.

  • Helping navigate online banking, utilities and essential services
  • Protecting against scams and suspicious emails or calls
  • Setting up devices so they are simple and easy to use
  • Being a calm, trusted point of contact for questions and concerns

Ongoing life optimisation

For people who want a long-term partner in simplifying and refining their life. Together, we design systems and rhythms that keep you in the driver's seat.

  • Weekly or fortnightly check-ins to set priorities and clear bottlenecks
  • Ongoing support across admin, tech, projects and planning
  • Thinking ahead so nothing important falls through the cracks
  • Helping you become the architect of a life that actually works for you

Packages & pricing

  • Email / phone / video support as needed within the booked time

Ideal if you want to try working together before committing to more.

Monthly support – Essentials

From $900/month

Steady support to keep life clear and moving.

Good for busy professionals, households and elders who want regular help.

Monthly support – Premium

From $1,800/month

For high performers who want a true right hand.

Ideal if you want someone embedded in your world over time.

Embedded & travel support
